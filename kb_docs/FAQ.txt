NOVATECH SOLUTIONS — EMPLOYEE IT & HR FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Document ID: GEN-FAQ-001 | Version 1.3 | Last Revised: March 2025
Maintained by: People Operations & IT Helpdesk

This document is used by the NovaTech L1 Support Engine (NTSE) to resolve
common employee queries without human intervention.

================================================================================
SECTION A: IT & ACCESS
================================================================================

Q: How do I reset my NovaTech password?
A: Use the self-service portal at sso.novatech.in/reset from any device. Enter your
registered email (firstname.lastname@novatech.in), verify via OTP to your registered
mobile, and set a new password. Password must be at least 12 characters with 1 uppercase,
1 lowercase, 1 number, and 1 special character. Cannot reuse last 10 passwords.
If SSPR fails, visit IT Help Desk at Floor 2, Block B, Bengaluru HQ with your employee ID.

Q: My account is locked. What do I do?
A: Accounts auto-unlock after 30 minutes. For immediate unlock call +91-80-4567-8901.
Accounts lock after 5 consecutive failed login attempts. If you did not cause the lockout,
report immediately to security@novatech.in as a potential account compromise.

Q: How do I install the VPN?
A: Download Cisco AnyConnect from software.novatech.in. Install and enter vpn.novatech.in
as the server address. Log in with your NovaTech SSO credentials and MFA token.
Select NovaTech-Standard profile for regular access. VPN uses SSL-TLS on port 443.

Q: How do I request access to a new system or tool?
A: Submit an IT-ACC-REQ form at support.novatech.in/access-request. Include the system
name, access level, business justification, and duration. Manager approval required within
2 business days. Classified systems (AWS prod, finance databases) require additional
IT Security review. Access provisioned within 1 business day of final approval.

Q: Can I install software that is not in the approved catalogue?
A: Yes, but you need approval. Raise an IT-SWR ticket at support.novatech.in with the
software name, version, and business justification. Standard requests take 3 business days.
Security-reviewed requests (AI tools, network tools) take 7 business days.
Do not install unapproved software — it is a violation of the Acceptable Use Policy.

Q: What laptop am I entitled to?
A: L1-L4 employees receive a Dell Latitude 5540 or HP EliteBook 845 with docking station,
keyboard, mouse, and 24-inch monitor. L5-L7 receive Dell XPS 15 or MacBook Pro 14-inch
with dual 27-inch monitors. L8 and above receive MacBook Pro 16-inch or ThinkPad X1 Carbon
with triple monitor setup. Laptops are refreshed every 3 years.

Q: My laptop is damaged. How do I get it replaced?
A: First occurrence of accidental damage is replaced at no cost after manager approval.
Submit IT-HW-REPLACE form at support.novatech.in. Repeat damage may require employee to
bear 20-50% of replacement cost. For theft or loss, file an FIR with local authorities
within 24 hours and submit FIR copy plus IT-HW-LOSS form within 48 hours.
IT provisions replacement within 3 business days.

Q: What are the helpdesk hours?
A: Monday to Friday 8AM to 9PM IST. Saturday 9AM to 5PM IST.
Sunday and public holidays: on-call only. For critical production outages (SEV-1):
call +91-80-4567-8901 available 24x7.

Q: How do I raise an IT support ticket?
A: Go to support.novatech.in, log in with SSO credentials, click New Ticket, select
category (Hardware / Software / Network / Access / Other), fill in title, description,
and severity, then submit. You will receive a ticket ID (format NT-XXXXXX) within 5 minutes.
Do not email individual IT team members — all requests must go through the portal.

Q: What are the ticket severity levels and response times?
A: SEV-1 Critical (production down, data loss risk): 15-minute response.
SEV-2 High (major feature unavailable, more than 10 users impacted): 2-hour response.
SEV-3 Medium (single user impacted, workaround available): 8 business hours.
SEV-4 Low (general query, minor inconvenience): 24 business hours.

Q: How do I report a phishing email?
A: Forward the email to phishing@novatech.in and do not click any links in it.
Do not attempt to investigate yourself. If you accidentally clicked a link, call
the Security Hotline immediately at +91-80-4567-8902 (24x7). You will not face
disciplinary action for accidental clicks if you report promptly.

Q: Can I use personal USB drives on my work laptop?
A: No. Connecting personal USB devices to company laptops without IT approval is
prohibited under the Acceptable Use Policy. Raise a ticket if you need to transfer
files — IT can provide approved methods such as SharePoint or OneDrive.

Q: What happens to my account and data when I resign?
A: Your access is revoked on your last working day at 6PM IST for voluntary resignation.
Email is archived for 90 days and files retained for 1 year. You must return all hardware
(laptop, accessories, access cards) on or before your last working day.

SECTION B: LEAVE & ATTENDANCE
================================================================================

Q: How many leave days do I get per year?
A: Privileged Leave (PL): 18 days — can carry forward up to 30 days, encashable up to
10 days at year-end. Sick Leave (SL): 12 days — requires medical certificate for more
than 2 consecutive days, cannot be carried forward. Casual Leave (CL): 6 days — cannot
be combined with PL or public holidays, cannot be carried forward.
Maternity Leave: 26 weeks. Paternity Leave: 10 days within 3 months of birth or adoption.
Bereavement Leave: 5 days for immediate family.

Q: How do I apply for leave?
A: Log in to Workday at workday.novatech.in. Navigate to Time Off > Request Time Off.
Select leave type, dates, and reason (optional for PL, mandatory for SL over 2 days).
Apply at least 24 hours in advance except for emergencies. Manager must act on the
request within 1 business day.

Q: Can I carry forward unused leaves to next year?
A: Only Privileged Leave (PL) can be carried forward, up to a maximum of 30 days.
Sick Leave and Casual Leave cannot be carried forward and lapse at year-end.

Q: What happens if I exhaust all my leaves?
A: Additional absences are treated as Leave Without Pay (LWP) and salary is deducted
proportionally. LWP for more than 3 consecutive days requires HR approval.
Apply via People Operations portal.

Q: How do I check my leave balance?
A: Log in to Workday at workday.novatech.in. Navigate to Time Off > Leave Balance.
Balances update automatically when leaves are approved.

Q: What is the WFH policy?
A: Engineering and Product employees: minimum 2 days in office per week (Tuesday and
Thursday recommended), maximum 3 days WFH. Sales and Client Success: 3 days in office.
HR, Finance, Operations: 3 days in office. Interns and Trainees: 4 days in office,
WFH only on Fridays. Submit WFH requests in Workday under Time Off > Work From Home
at least 1 day in advance.

SECTION C: PAYROLL & EXPENSES
================================================================================

Q: When is salary credited?
A: Salaries are processed on the last working day of each month via NEFT/IMPS.
Payroll cut-off for attendance and leave data is the 25th of each month.

Q: Where can I find my payslip?
A: Log in to Workday at workday.novatech.in. Navigate to Pay > Payslips.
Payslips are available from the 1st of each month for the previous month.

Q: My salary is incorrect this month. What do I do?
A: Raise a ticket at support.novatech.in with category HR > Payroll.
Include the payslip month and the specific discrepancy (amount, component).
SLA for payroll queries is 3 business days. You can also email payroll@novatech.in.

Q: When is Form 16 issued?
A: Form 16 (Part A and Part B) is issued by June 15 each year for the preceding
financial year. Available in Workday under Pay > Tax Documents.

Q: When does the investment declaration window open?
A: Investment declaration window opens April 1 and closes December 31.
Final proof of investment submission is due February 28.
Missing the deadline results in excess TDS deduction in the March salary.

Q: How do I claim a reimbursement?
A: Log in to Workday > Expenses > Create Expense Report. Add line items with date,
amount, category, and business justification. Attach scanned receipts (JPG or PDF).
Receipts are mandatory for claims above Rs. 500. Submit for manager approval.
Manager must approve within 3 business days. Finance processes in the next payroll cycle.
Claims must be submitted within 30 days of incurring the expense.

Q: What is the internet reimbursement amount for WFH employees?
A: Rs. 1,000 per month. Submit via Workday > Expenses with your internet bill as receipt.

Q: What is the mobile phone allowance?
A: L1 to L4 employees receive Rs. 750 per month. L5 and above receive Rs. 1,500 per month.
Claimed via Workday > Expenses monthly.

SECTION D: HR & GRIEVANCES
================================================================================

Q: How do I raise a workplace grievance?
A: You can raise a grievance via the online portal at grievance.novatech.in (supports
anonymous submissions), by email to grievance@novatech.in, or via physical sealed envelope
deposited in drop-boxes on each floor addressed to HR Grievance Cell, People Operations.
Acknowledgement is sent within 1 business day for named submissions.

Q: What is the grievance escalation path?
A: Level 1: Informal conversation with immediate manager (within 5 working days).
Level 2: HR Business Partner at hrbp@novatech.in (resolves within 15 days).
Level 3: Formal Grievance Committee — hearing within 10 days, resolution within 30 days.
Level 4: HR Director at hrdirector@novatech.in — decision within 15 days, binding.
Level 5: External — State Labour Commissioner or Labour Court.

Q: How do I report sexual harassment?
A: Contact the Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) directly at posh-icc@novatech.in.
ICC Presiding Officer is Priya Nair (People Operations VP).
Complaints must be filed within 3 months of the incident (extendable to 6 months).
ICC has 90 days to complete inquiry. All proceedings are strictly confidential.
Do not use the general grievance portal for POSH complaints.

Q: What is the notice period if I resign?
A: L1 to L4: 30 days. L5 to L7: 60 days. L8 and above: 90 days.
Notice period can be bought out — L1-L4 requires manager approval, L5-L7 requires
manager and HR approval, L8+ requires CHRO approval and is rarely granted.
Submit resignation formally via Workday under Profile > Job > Resign.
Verbal resignations are not accepted.

Q: When do I receive my Full and Final settlement after resignation?
A: Full and Final settlement is processed within 45 days of your last working day.
It includes pending salary, encashable leave balance, gratuity (if eligible — requires
5 or more years of service), minus any deductions for outstanding advances or
unreturned assets.

Q: How do I report financial fraud or ethical concerns?
A: Email ethics@novatech.in or call the Ethics Hotline at 1800-XXX-XXXX (toll-free, 24x7,
anonymous). The Ethics Committee chaired by the CFO reviews all complaints within 30 days.
Whistleblowers are protected from retaliation under NovaTech's Whistleblower Policy.
