NOVATECH SOLUTIONS — ESCALATION MATRIX & CONTACTS DIRECTORY
Document ID: GEN-ESC-001 | Version 3.0 | Last Revised: March 2025
Maintained by: IT Infrastructure & People Operations

This document defines ownership, escalation paths, SLA commitments, and contact
details for all support categories. Used by the NovaTech L1 Support Engine (NTSE)
to route tickets accurately and provide employees with the correct escalation contact.

================================================================================
SECTION 1: IT SUPPORT ESCALATION MATRIX
================================================================================

CATEGORY: Hardware (Laptops, Monitors, Peripherals, Access Cards)
  L1 Owner:     IT Helpdesk — support.novatech.in | helpdesk@novatech.in
  L2 Escalation: IT Hardware Team — ithardware@novatech.in
  L3 Escalation: IT Manager — itmanager@novatech.in
  SLA L1:       SEV-2: 2 hours | SEV-3: 8 business hours | SEV-4: 24 business hours
  Forms:        IT-HW-REPLACE (damage/replacement) | IT-HW-LOSS (theft/loss)
  Notes:        Loaner devices available for SEV-2 hardware failures within 4 business hours.
                Physical damage assessment done by IT Hardware Team in person.

CATEGORY: Software (Installation, Licensing, Application Errors)
  L1 Owner:     IT Helpdesk — support.novatech.in
  L2 Escalation: IT Software Team — itsoftware@novatech.in
  L3 Escalation: IT Manager — itmanager@novatech.in
  SLA L1:       SEV-3: 8 business hours | SEV-4: 24 business hours
  Forms:        IT-SWR (software installation request requiring approval)
  Approved Software Portal: software.novatech.in
  Notes:        AI/ML tools and GPL-licensed software require security review (7 business days).

CATEGORY: Network & VPN (Connectivity, VPN, Wi-Fi, Firewall)
  L1 Owner:     IT Helpdesk — support.novatech.in
  L2 Escalation: IT Network Team — network@novatech.in
  L3 Escalation: IT Infrastructure Lead — infra@novatech.in
  SLA L1:       SEV-1: 15 minutes | SEV-2: 2 hours | SEV-3: 8 business hours
  VPN Server:   vpn.novatech.in | Protocol: SSL-TLS | Port: 443
  Notes:        VPN issues affecting >10 employees simultaneously = SEV-1, escalate directly
                to network@novatech.in without waiting for L1 resolution attempt.

CATEGORY: Access Management (Account Provisioning, SSO, MFA, Permissions)
  L1 Owner:     IT Helpdesk — support.novatech.in | sso.novatech.in/reset (self-service)
  L2 Escalation: IT Access Management Team — iam@novatech.in
  L3 Escalation: IT Security — security@novatech.in (for suspected compromises)
  SLA L1:       SEV-2: 2 hours | SEV-3: 8 business hours
  Forms:        IT-ACC-REQ (new access request)
  Notes:        Suspected account compromise escalates immediately to security@novatech.in
                bypassing L1. Access reviews happen quarterly — unused access auto-revoked
                after 60 days with 7-day notice.

CATEGORY: Security Incidents (Phishing, Malware, Data Breach, Lost Device)
  L1 Owner:     Security Hotline — +91-80-4567-8902 (24x7 for SEV-1/SEV-2)
  L2 Escalation: IT Security Team — security@novatech.in
  L3 Escalation: CISO — ciso@novatech.in
  SLA:          SEV-1: 15 minutes (call hotline, do not raise portal ticket)
  Phishing:     Forward to phishing@novatech.in — do not click links
  Notes:        Employees who report promptly are protected under Safe Harbour policy.
                Do not attempt self-remediation. Isolate affected device immediately.

CATEGORY: Microsoft 365 (Outlook, Teams, SharePoint, OneDrive)
  L1 Owner:     IT Helpdesk — support.novatech.in
  L2 Escalation: IT Software Team — itsoftware@novatech.in
  L3 Escalation: Microsoft Support via IT (enterprise contract)
  SLA L1:       SEV-3: 8 business hours
  Service Status: status.office.com (check before raising ticket)
  Notes:        M365 outages affecting all employees are Microsoft-side — check
                status.office.com first. Account-specific issues handled by IT.

SECTION 2: HR SUPPORT ESCALATION MATRIX
================================================================================

CATEGORY: Leave & Attendance
  L1 Owner:     Workday self-service — workday.novatech.in
  L2 Escalation: HR Business Partner — hrbp@novatech.in
  L3 Escalation: HR Director — hrdirector@novatech.in
  SLA L2:       Acknowledgement within 2 business days, resolution within 7 business days
  Notes:        Leave balance queries, application errors, attendance corrections
                handled by HRBP. Policy clarifications available via NTSE.

CATEGORY: Payroll & Compensation
  L1 Owner:     Workday self-service (payslips, tax documents) — workday.novatech.in
  L2 Escalation: Payroll Team — payroll@novatech.in
                 Ticket: support.novatech.in > HR > Payroll
  L3 Escalation: Finance Operations — finance-ops@novatech.in
  SLA L2:       3 business days
  Notes:        Always include payslip month and specific discrepancy amount in ticket.
                Payroll cut-off is 25th of each month.

CATEGORY: Reimbursements & Expenses
  L1 Owner:     Workday self-service — workday.novatech.in > Expenses
  L2 Escalation: Finance Operations — finance-ops@novatech.in
  SLA L2:       3 business days
  Notes:        Claims must be submitted within 30 days of expense. Late claims (>30 days)
                require Finance Head approval.

CATEGORY: Workplace Grievances
  L1 Owner:     grievance.novatech.in | grievance@novatech.in (anonymous supported)
  L2 Escalation: HR Business Partner — hrbp@novatech.in
  L3 Escalation: Grievance Committee (3-member panel) — constituted within 7 days
  L4 Escalation: HR Director — hrdirector@novatech.in (binding decision)
  L5 Escalation: External — State Labour Commissioner / Labour Court
  SLA:          HRBP acknowledgement: 2 business days | HRBP resolution: 15 business days
                Grievance Committee resolution: 30 business days
  Notes:        Retaliation against grievance filers is a Code of Conduct violation.

CATEGORY: Sexual Harassment (POSH)
  Owner:        Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) — posh-icc@novatech.in
  ICC Head:     Priya Nair, VP People Operations — priya.nair@novatech.in
  External Member: Adv. Sunita Rao
  SLA:          ICC inquiry completion: 90 days from complaint receipt
  Notes:        POSH complaints must NOT be routed through general grievance portal.
                File directly with ICC. Anonymous complaints accepted but limit investigation.
                Interim relief (transfer, leave) available during investigation.

CATEGORY: Onboarding & Offboarding
  L1 Owner:     People Operations — peopleops@novatech.in
  L2 Escalation: HR Business Partner — hrbp@novatech.in
  Notes:        New joiners: Day 1 issues (laptop not ready, access not provisioned)
                — contact IT Helpdesk AND People Operations simultaneously.
                Resignation: Submit via Workday > Profile > Job > Resign.
                F&F settlement within 45 days of last working day.

CATEGORY: Ethics & Whistleblower
  Owner:        Ethics Committee (chaired by CFO and Legal Counsel)
  Contact:      ethics@novatech.in | Ethics Hotline: 1800-XXX-XXXX (24x7, anonymous)
  SLA:          30 days for committee review
  Notes:        For financial misconduct, fraud, data privacy violations, ethical breaches.
                Whistleblowers protected from retaliation under Whistleblower Policy.
