NOVATECH SOLUTIONS — NEW EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE
Document ID: HR-OB-001 | Version 3.1 | Last Revised: March 2025
Maintained by: People Operations | peopleops@novatech.in

This guide covers everything a new NovaTech employee needs from the offer acceptance
stage through the first 90 days. It is also used by the NovaTech L1 Support Engine
(NTSE) to answer onboarding-related queries from new joiners and their managers.

================================================================================
SECTION 1: PRE-JOINING (BEFORE DAY 1)
================================================================================

OFFER LETTER & DOCUMENTATION
- Offer letter is sent via DocuSign to your personal email 5-7 days before joining date.
- Sign and return digitally. Do not print and scan — DocuSign signatures are legally binding.
- If you have not received your offer letter 3 days before joining, contact
  peopleops@novatech.in immediately.

BACKGROUND VERIFICATION
- Background verification is conducted via AuthBridge portal.
- Link is sent to your personal email within 24 hours of offer acceptance.
- Verification covers: identity, education, previous employment (last 3 employers),
  address, and criminal record check.
- Verification must be completed before Day 1 — incomplete BGV may delay onboarding.
- BGV typically takes 5-7 business days. You will receive a clearance confirmation email.
- For queries on BGV status: bgv@novatech.in

DOCUMENT SUBMISSION
Submit the following via NovaTech Document Portal (link sent to personal email):
- PAN card (mandatory for payroll setup)
- Aadhaar card
- Educational certificates (highest qualification + any certifications)
- Previous employment documents: last 3 months payslips, experience letters,
  relieving letter from most recent employer
- Bank account details: account number, IFSC code, cancelled cheque or bank statement
- Passport-size photograph (for employee ID card)
- Address proof (Aadhaar acceptable)

Failure to submit documents before Day 1 may delay salary processing for Month 1.
Contact peopleops@novatech.in if you are unable to submit any document.

DAY 1 WELCOME KIT EMAIL
You will receive a Welcome Kit email 2 days before joining containing:
- Office address and reporting instructions (report to People Operations at 9:30 AM)
- Building access procedure for Day 1 (visitor pass at Front Desk)
- Parking pass (if applicable — Bengaluru HQ only)
- IT setup guide (what to expect for laptop provisioning)
- Dress code reminder (business casual on Day 1 for induction; smart casual thereafter)
- Emergency contact numbers for Day 1

================================================================================
SECTION 2: DAY 1 INDUCTION
================================================================================

REPORTING & ARRIVAL
- Report to People Operations desk at 9:30 AM sharp on your joining date.
- Carry original copies of all submitted documents for physical verification.
- Collect employee ID card and building access card from Front Desk after HR check-in.
- Parking sticker (if applicable) collected from Front Desk with employee ID card.

IT SETUP (DAY 1 MORNING — conducted by IT Helpdesk)
- Collect your provisioned laptop from IT Help Desk (Floor 2, Block B, Bengaluru HQ).
- IT will walk you through:
  * Initial Windows login and mandatory password change
  * Cisco AnyConnect VPN setup (vpn.novatech.in)
  * Microsoft 365 activation (Outlook, Teams, OneDrive)
  * Slack workspace access (novatech-hq.slack.com)
  * MFA enrollment (Google Authenticator or hardware token)
  * CrowdStrike Falcon verification (must show active protection)
- Do not attempt to set up software yourself before IT walkthrough.
- If your laptop is not ready on Day 1, raise an urgent ticket via helpdesk@novatech.in
  — loaner device will be provided within 2 hours.

DAY 1 ACCESS PACKAGE (auto-provisioned)
All new employees receive the following access on Day 1:
- NovaTech SSO account (firstname.lastname@novatech.in)
- Microsoft 365 (Outlook, Teams, SharePoint, OneDrive)
- Slack workspace (novatech-hq.slack.com)
- NovaTech Intranet (intranet.novatech.in)
- Workday HRMS (workday.novatech.in) — for leave, payroll, expenses
- Jira & Confluence — project-level access granted by team lead within 2 business days
- GitHub Enterprise (github.novatech.in) — access granted by team lead

Role-specific access (AWS console, database access, Salesforce, etc.) must be
requested by your manager via IT-ACC-REQ form within your first week.

MANDATORY E-LEARNING MODULES (complete by end of Week 1)
The following modules must be completed on learn.novatech.in before end of first week:
1. Code of Conduct & Ethics (45 minutes) — covers acceptable behaviour, conflicts of interest
2. POSH Awareness Training (30 minutes) — mandatory under law
3. IT Security Basics (60 minutes) — password hygiene, phishing awareness, data handling
4. Data Privacy Fundamentals (45 minutes) — GDPR basics, data classification, handling rules
5. Information Security Policy Acknowledgement (15 minutes) — sign-off required

Failure to complete modules within 1 week triggers an automated reminder from People Ops.
Modules not completed within 2 weeks are escalated to your reporting manager.

TEAM INTRODUCTION
- Your reporting manager will have your first 1:1 scheduled on Day 1 or Day 2.
- A team introduction meeting is calendar-blocked by your manager in your first week.
- You will be assigned a buddy (peer from your team) to help you navigate the first 30 days.
  Buddy assignment is done by your manager before Day 1.

================================================================================
SECTION 3: FIRST 30 DAYS
================================================================================

WEEK 1 PRIORITIES
- Complete IT setup and all mandatory e-learning modules
- Set up Outlook, Teams, and Slack — ensure you can send and receive on all platforms
- Meet your reporting manager for role briefing and initial goal-setting
- Connect with your buddy for informal guidance on team processes and culture
- Review this onboarding guide and the NovaTech Employee Handbook on intranet.novatech.in
- Request any role-specific system access via IT-ACC-REQ (your manager will guide you)

WEEK 2-4 PRIORITIES
- Begin working on starter tasks assigned by your manager
- Complete any pending document submissions
- Attend team rituals (standups, sprint ceremonies, team meetings)
- Schedule introductory 1:1s with key stakeholders your manager identifies
- Raise any access blockers immediately via support.novatech.in — do not wait

30-DAY CHECK-IN
A formal 30-day check-in is conducted by your reporting manager. Topics covered:
- Role clarity: are your responsibilities and goals clear?
- Access and tools: do you have everything you need?
- Initial feedback: how is the onboarding experience?
- Blockers: anything preventing you from being effective?
- Culture fit: how are you finding the team and environment?

This is a two-way conversation — you are expected to give honest feedback.
Document is filed with HR by your manager after the meeting.

================================================================================
SECTION 4: 30 TO 60 DAYS
================================================================================

MID-PROBATION REVIEW (DAY 60)
Conducted by your HR Business Partner (HRBP), not your manager.
Purpose: independent check on your experience, separate from manager feedback.
Topics:
- Performance and contribution so far
- Culture and team integration
- Any concerns you want to raise confidentially with HR
- Wellbeing and workload check

HRBP contact: hrbp@novatech.in
You can also proactively reach out to your HRBP at any time during probation.

WHAT YOU SHOULD HAVE DONE BY DAY 60
- All mandatory e-learning completed (overdue by now if not done)
- All system access set up and functional
- First independent deliverable or contribution made
- Probation goals clearly defined and agreed with manager in writing
- Expense reimbursement account set up in Workday if applicable

================================================================================
SECTION 5: 60 TO 90 DAYS — PROBATION COMPLETION
================================================================================

PROBATION PERIOD OVERVIEW
- Standard probation: 90 days for most roles (L1-L6)
- Extended probation: 180 days for senior positions (L7 and above)
- Probation can be extended by 30 days once with written justification from manager
  and HR approval. Employee must be notified before probation end date.

PROBATION COMPLETION REVIEW (DAY 90)
Conducted jointly by reporting manager and HR Business Partner.
Topics:
- Performance against probation goals
- Conduct and adherence to policies
- Team feedback (manager may collect 2-3 peer inputs)
- Confirmation decision

CONFIRMATION OUTCOMES
- Confirmed: Confirmation letter issued via Workday within 5 business days of review.
  Salary revision (if applicable for confirmed employees) effective from Day 91.
- Extended: Written communication with specific improvement areas and new review date.
  Extension cannot exceed 30 days beyond original 90-day period.
- Not confirmed (separation): Rare outcome. Governed by Employment Agreement terms.
  Notice period during probation is 15 days (both sides).

WHAT CHANGES AFTER CONFIRMATION
- Full leave entitlements activate (some leave types are pro-rated during probation)
- Eligible for internal transfer requests after 6 months post-confirmation
- Performance appraisal cycle inclusion from next APR (April)
- Eligible to nominate for L&D courses requiring confirmation status

SECTION 6: COMMON NEW JOINER QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: My laptop was not ready on Day 1. What do I do?
A: Contact IT Helpdesk immediately at helpdesk@novatech.in or +91-80-4567-8901.
   A loaner device will be provisioned within 2 hours. Raise a ticket at support.novatech.in
   with category Hardware > New Joiner Laptop Not Provisioned.

Q: I did not receive my SSO account credentials before Day 1.
A: Your SSO account is created on your joining date by People Operations.
   If not received by 10 AM on Day 1, contact peopleops@novatech.in or visit
   the People Operations desk directly.

Q: I cannot access Jira or Confluence.
A: Jira and Confluence project-level access is granted by your team lead within
   2 business days of joining. If not received by Day 3, ask your team lead to
   submit an IT-ACC-REQ form at support.novatech.in/access-request.

Q: My salary was not credited for my first month.
A: First-month salary is processed in the regular payroll cycle (last working day of month).
   If you joined after the 25th payroll cut-off, your first month salary is included
   in the following month's payroll (you will receive 2 months salary together).
   For queries: raise ticket at support.novatech.in > HR > Payroll or email payroll@novatech.in.

Q: I submitted my documents but got an error on the portal.
A: Email peopleops@novatech.in with the document attached directly.
   Include your full name, employee ID (if already issued), and joining date.

Q: I need to apply for leave during my probation period. Is that allowed?
A: Yes. Leave entitlements apply from Day 1, though Casual Leave is not available
   during the first 30 days of employment. Sick leave is available from Day 1.
   Privileged Leave accrues from Day 1 but is subject to manager approval.
   Apply via Workday at workday.novatech.in > Time Off > Request Time Off.

Q: Who is my HR Business Partner?
A: HRBP assignments are by business unit. Your manager will introduce you to your
   HRBP at or before your Day 30 check-in. You can also email hrbp@novatech.in
   to find out who is assigned to your team.

Q: Can I work from home during my first month?
A: Interns and Trainees: WFH only on Fridays, must be in office 4 days per week.
   All other roles: standard WFH policy applies (Engineering: min 2 days in office).
   However, managers may request new joiners to be in office more frequently during
   the first 30 days for onboarding purposes. Follow your manager's guidance.

Q: When does my health insurance coverage start?
A: Group health insurance enrollment is completed by People Operations within your
   first week. Coverage typically activates from your date of joining.
   Insurance details and policy documents are available on intranet.novatech.in > Benefits.
   For enrollment queries: benefits@novatech.in
